Kitchen Staples

Right now, there is more awareness than ever before around health and fitness. People have become more educated on the dangers of unhealthy eating and poor fitness levels. With that, people have taken action to take better care of themselves.
A huge part of this is understanding the foods you need to nourish your body to help it function to its full capacity. Everyone is so different, so there are endless possibilities out there for meals and snacks. That being said, whether you're a veggie, vegan or keto-warrior, there are certain staples that are must-haves in the kitchen to make it all that little bit easier:

Pantry Staples

  • Oil - I generally like Olive Oil or Sunflower Oil!
  • Spices - Everyone should have at least the following:
    1. Salt and Pepper of course!
    2. Chilli Powder
    3. Tumeric
    4. Paprika
    5. Cajun Spice
    6. Chilli Flakes
    7. Curry Powder
    8. Oregano
    9. Parsley
    10. Rosemary
    11. Thyme
  • Rice
  • Pasta
  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Pasata
  • Tomato Puree
  • Soy Sauce
  • Stock (chicken or vegetable)

Fresh Produce

  • Of course at the top of this list is: Garlic!
  • Onions (to cook with your garlic)
  • Fresh veggies - I like carrots and peppers, but to each their own!

Gadgets

  • First and foremost: A non-stick pan!
  • Heat resistant spatula
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Good quality knives
  • Chopping board
  • Tin opener
  • Garlic press
  • Cheese grater
  • Vegetable Peeler
  • Weighing scales
  • A great caserole dish

And there you have it folks, now that you've kitted out your kitchen, you're ready to get yourself cookin!
